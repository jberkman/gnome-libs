Standard Source Annotation Format:

@BEGIN-NOTES@
<notes>
	<author>
		<name></name>
		<email></email>
		<contribution></contribution>
	</author>
	<todo>
	</todo>
	<bugs>
		<historical>
		...
		</historical>
		<current>
		...
		</current>
	</bugs>
</notes>
@END-NOTES@

Using this in your source & header files (preferably in a comment
immediately after the copyright/license) will allow more people to
maintain the software easily.
